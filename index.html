About Us

Dombivli's favourite spot to play, eat & chill

The Gamer's Hideout is a youth-friendly gaming café right near Pendharkar College in Dombivli. We're the best place for gaming, food and hangouts – whether you're here for console, pool or party gaming.

Come for the games, stay for the pizzas, mocktails and vibes. Book a slot, grab your squad and make it your regular hideout.
